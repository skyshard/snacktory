27 Perfect Graduation 2017 Gift Ideas

See 27 gift ideas that are perfect for the new graduate in your life, from an Airbnb gift card to an interior design service.

Emma Sarran Webster

Graduation season is officially in full swing as we head toward summer; some people have already thrown their caps in the air, and others are eagerly preparing to do so in the next few weeks. If you’re one of them, chances are you’ll have at least a few people who want to reward your achievements with a gift of some sort — and some might even ask you what you want. Sure, it can feel awkward requesting gifts sometimes, but hey, you’ve worked hard — and if a loved one insists on gifting you something, wouldn’t it be nice if it’s something you’ll really use? Not quite sure what that would be? Here are 27 suggestions, in just about every price range, that are perfect for that post-grad life.
